Fried Chicken

Description

Fried Chicken is a very simple dish that anyone can cook. It is basically chicken meat parts that are fried in medium-high heat. Despite it's simplicity, there is so many ways to prepare this dish that makes each recipe taste unique!

In this recipe, I will share the way I like to prepare my fried chicken!

Ingredients

  • ¾ kg chicken thighs
  • 1 whole garlic
  • 1 whole onion
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon rosemarry
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 teaspon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Ajinomoto MSG
  • 15 calamansi
  • Olive oil
  • Canola oil
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspon baking soda
  • 30g-packet Crispy Fry Breading Mix

Steps

  1. Wash chicken thighs with running water.
  2. Use a fork to poke holes into the chicken thighs then set aside in a mixing bowl.
  3. Mince the garlic and onions.
  4. Drizzle olive oil on the chicken thighs until fully coated.
  5. Add the onions, garlic, basil, thyme, rosemarry, paprika, garlic powder, Knorr Liquid Seasoning, Ajinomoto MSG, salt and pepper to the bowl.
  6. Squeeze and strain the calamansi and drizzle the juices over the chicken.
  7. Mix everything in the bowl until the chicken thighs are evenly coated.
  8. Cover bowl with clingwrap and keep it refrigated for at least 8 hours.
  9. Take the bowl of chicken thighs out of the refrigerator.
  10. Mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder and the breading mix in another bowl.
  11. Coat the chicken in the dry batter and let it rest.
  12. Heat up oil in a pan.
  13. After 3 minutes, do a second coating of breading to the chicken thighs.
  14. Once the oil is hot, deep fry the chicken thighs until golden brown.
  15. Take out the chicken thighs and let them cool on a cooling rack.
  16. Let the fried chicken thighs rest for 5 minutes and then serve with rice or mashed potatoes. Enjoy!
