Tortang Talong

Description

Tortang Talong is a simple Filipino dish that only has 2 primary ingredients: Eggplant and Egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 egg
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Clean the eggplant with running water.
  2. Beat the egg in a bowl.
  3. Add a pinch of salt to the beaten eggs.
  4. Charr the skin of the eggplant over an open flame.
  5. Place the eggplant in a bowl with ice cold water.
  6. Peel off the eggplant skin after it has cooled off.
  7. Mash the eggplant on a flat tray with a fork. Make sure it doesn't break off from the stem.
  8. Pour the beaten eggs over the eggplant until covered.
  9. Heat up cooking oil in a pan.
  10. Fry the eggplant and egg mixture until golden brown.
  11. Serve the tortang talong with rice and ketchup. Enjoy!
